Columbus Design Biennial

The Columbus Design Biennial will pair the historically significant architecture of Columbus, Indiana with the best designers of today for site-specific installations across the city. Its goal is to continue the 70+ year conversation between Columbus and the best makers of the time. 100 Variations: New Reflections on Eliel Saarinen and the Golden Ratio by Jonathan Nesci is designed to serve as a prototype for many future exhibitions to come.
