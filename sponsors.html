Made Possible by the Generosity of

The Haddad Foundation

The Haddad Foundation was established to support charitable programming benefiting central Indiana residents, focusing in Bartholomew, Hamilton, and Marion counties. Since 2002, the Haddad Foundation’s charitable contributions throughout Indiana have helped numerous charitable organizations.
